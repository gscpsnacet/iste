HOW TO REGISTER



Instructions to the Participants

On receiving the intimation of acceptance, participants are requested to send the filled in registration form to the organizing chair with a DD drawn in favor of “The Principal, PSNA College of Engineering and Technology,” payable at Dindigul, Tamilnadu, India. The fee includes the conference kit, lunch and refreshment. The completed registration form and DD must reach on or before 20.02.2017. Registration form can be downloaded from www.psnacet.edu.in.

Submission Details

 The papers can be submitted through email: iste@psnacet.edu.in. Submitted papers must be original and unpublished. Papers must be written in English should be at most 4 pages long in total and must conform to IEEE format to IEEE format. IEEE format can be downloaded from the conference website.

Accommodation:

Accommodation for student delegates and authors shall be arranged in our hostel. Hostel Accommodation on payment basis can be availed, on request.
